  <p>Built a hardware-agnostic Windows imaging solution to streamline workstation deployment across multiple hardware generations and locations. Developed images in a virtualized build environment, baking in applications, security policies, domain configurations, and user-specific settings to eliminate manual setup at the point of deployment. Coordinated with an external technical support vendor to ensure proper implementation of software dependencies and licensing requirements. Designed secure VPN and authentication protocols to support remote users, and continuously refined staging configurations and deployment workflows to keep provisioning consistent and maintainable as the environment evolved across multiple sites. Developed working documentation for organizational and TSP use.</p>
